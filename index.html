    • "happy years;" Is a true love story of oneself and friendship between three girls, Belladonna "Donna", Cornelius "Corie", And Izadora "Izzy". Belladonna Absinthe enters in as the new, very young, CEO and the fashion industry doesnt know what to think. These three women who are just trying to survive the competitive career at a editorial company for the infamous "St. Laurent Magazine". Not only that, there is danger and speculation of missing women within Khan City and the modeling industry. Personal health, growth, and the outside world clammering down on the three women, all they really want is some mamosas and some mani-pedis!

    "happy years;" Is meant to be a heartfelt story, of not only the love the three women will share for each other later, but each individual will represent a core value of mental health and emotional health. Its important in our society that we pass on knowledgable values, even through fiction like stories. "Happy years;" with myself and my co-creator, Odette, thought that being able to pass important messages on important subjects, such as; LGBTQ community, racism, prejudices, abuse, etc, will be able to suggest to start a productive conversation. The material may trigger you, so we gently advise that before reading the one season series "happy years;" that you may seek advice from a close friend, close parent, or anyone you closely trust. We will always offer support through fan community, because anything helps, and we fully understand the sensitivity of each subject.
